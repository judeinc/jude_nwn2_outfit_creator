Jude NWN2 Outfit Creator v1.2.4A
Hotfix release for v1.2.4

This is a minor hotfix package for the first public v1.2.4 release. It keeps
the add-on version at 1.2.4, but the release package is marked with an "A"
suffix so users can tell it includes the fitting workflow fix.

Fixes
- Fixed Step 4 restore-scale fitting adjustments so shift, vertical shift,
  tilt, scale, and profile-shape object movement keep the weighted outfit and
  Ai_Rig moving together as a stable fitted pair.
- Prevented Step 4 adjustment buttons from moving Ai_Rig as a live deformer in
  a way that could offset the armature relationship before Step 5 baking.
- Added safeguards so in-progress scenes that still have newModel_Mesh parented
  to Ai_Rig are normalized before final fitting.

Verification
- Confirmed the add-on compiles under Python.
- Confirmed Blender 5.1 can register and unregister the add-on.
- Confirmed repeated Step 4 adjustment operations leave vertex-group weights
  unchanged.
- Confirmed the mesh-to-Ai_Rig relative transform remains stable while the pair
  is moved into final NWN2 scale/position.
