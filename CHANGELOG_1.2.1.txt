Jude's AI to NwN2 Outfit Creator
Version 1.2.1 Changelog

Summary
- Patch release on top of 1.2.0.
- Adds final fitting quality-of-life controls.
- Adds an optional vanilla body sculpting reference for HHM and HHF workflows.

Step 4 Final Scale/Fit
- Added Shift Forward and Shift Back controls.
- Shift controls move the fitted Ai_Rig in small 0.01 increments.
- The parented outfit mesh follows the rig, so the whole fitted output moves together.
- If the mesh is not parented to Ai_Rig, the operator moves both the rig and mesh as a fallback.
- Step 4 now tracks the current forward shift value and resets it to 0.000 when Restore to NWN2 Scale is run.
- Added an in-panel reminder that users may enter Pose Mode to repose the final output before clicking Done.

Step 5 Final Fitting / Sculpting
- Added a Show Vanilla Body / Hide Vanilla Body toggle below Sculpt / Elastic Deform.
- The toggle shows the selected profile's vanilla body as a sculpting reference:
  - HHM shows P_HHM_CL_Body01
  - HHF shows P_HHF_CL_Body01
- The inactive profile body is hidden automatically.
- Vanilla body reference objects stay hidden from render.
- The vanilla body no longer uses Show In Front, so it behaves like normal viewport geometry.
- The vanilla body is automatically hidden again when moving forward to:
  - Step 5 Done
  - Step 6 material setup/name building
  - Step 7 bake
  - Substance Painter handoff
  - Step 8 cleanup
  - MDB export

Notes
- This patch is intended to make final fitting easier for both male and female outfits.
- The vanilla body reference is viewport-only and should not affect baking, cleanup, or export.
